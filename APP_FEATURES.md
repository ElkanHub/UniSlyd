# Unislyd - APP User Guide & Feature Overview

**Turn your lecture slides into an AI you can study with.**

Welcome to **Unislyd**! This application is designed specifically for university students to make studying from lecture slides easier, faster, and more effective. Instead of scrolling through hundreds of slides to find one answer, simply upload your deck and ask Unislyd to find it for you.

---

## üöÄ Key Features

### 1. Smart Slide Upload
Stop manually searching through files. Unislyd ingests your lecture materials and understands them.
- **Supports PPTX & PDF**: Upload your PowerPoint presentations or PDF lecture notes directly.
- **Intelligent Extraction**: The system extracts text, bullet points, and structure from your slides, organizing them so the AI understands the context of every slide.

### 2. Conversational Study Buddy (AI Chat)
Chat with your slides as if they were a tutor.
- **Natural Language Queries**: Ask questions like "What did the professor say about Mitochondria?" or "Summarize the key points of Slide 3."
- **Context-Aware Answers**: Unislyd doesn't just guess. It scans your uploaded slides to find the *exact* information you need and generates an answer based *only* on your class material.
- **Source Grounding**: Trust the answer. The AI implicitly uses your lecture content to ensure accuracy, reducing hallucinations common in generic chatbots.

### 3. Study Organization (Dashboard)
Keep your courses and materials organized.
- **Deck Management**: View all your uploaded slide decks in one place. See file names, upload dates, and slide counts.
- **Conversation History**: All your study sessions are saved. Resume a previous chat to review for an exam or start a new one for a new topic.

### 4. Export & Review
- **PDF Export**: Save your AI-generated study guides or Q&A sessions as clean, readable PDFs. Perfect for printing or offline revision.
- **Editable Answers**: Need to tweak a note? You can edit the AI's responses directly in the chat interface before exporting.

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I use this for any subject?**
A: Yes! As long as you have lecture slides (PPTX or PDF) with text, Unislyd can learn it. It works great for Biology, History, Law, Business, Computer Science, and more.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes. Your uploaded slides are processed securely and are only accessible by you.

**Q: How accurate is the AI?**
A: Unislyd is "grounded" in your data. Unlike ChatGPT which uses the entire internet, Unislyd prioritizes the information inside the files you upload, making it much more accurate for specific course material.
